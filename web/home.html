Vision

At SkillNest, we envision a world where every child, regardless of their abilities, can explore their creativity and imagination through crafting. Together, we’re crafting a brighter, more inclusive future for all.

Activities

Glass Art

Create beautiful glass art pieces using safe, child-friendly materials.
This activity encourages creativity and fine motor skills.
Requirements: No prior experience needed.
Outcome: Children will take home a unique glass art creation and learn about color blending and design.

Calligraphy

Learn the art of beautiful handwriting with beginner-friendly calligraphy techniques.
This activity promotes focus and patience.
Requirements: No prior experience needed. All tools and materials provided.
Outcome: Children will create their own calligraphy artwork and develop fine motor skills.

Wood Working

Discover the joy of wood working with easy-to-follow instructions.
This activity enhances hand-eye coordination and creativity.
Requirements: No prior experience needed. All materials provided.
Outcome: Children will complete a small wood project, such as a sculpture.

Planting

Explore the wonders of nature by planting and caring for your own mini garden.
This activity teaches responsibility and environmental awareness.
Requirements: No prior experience needed. All materials provided.
Outcome: Children will take home a potted plant and learn basic gardening skills.

Pottery

Shape and mold clay into unique pottery pieces.
This activity encourages creativity and tactile exploration.
Requirements: No prior experience needed. All materials provided.
Outcome: Children will create a handcrafted pottery item and learn about texture and form.

Sewing

Learn basic sewing techniques to create fun and functional items.
This activity promotes patience and problem-solving skills.
Requirements: No prior experience needed. All materials provided.
Outcome: Children will complete a small sewing project, such as a pouch or stuffed toy.
